# Introduction

Legend of the Five Rings is an RPG which began as a collectible card game, originally published by Alderac Entertainment Group under license from Five Rings Publishing Group, the company that held the L5R property and was itself spun off from the AEG and Isomedia partnership and which was later absorbed by Wizards of the Coast, which also bought TSR (the makers of Dungeons & Dragons) and is itself owned by Hasbro, which sold the rights to L5R back to Alderac, which later sold them to Fantasy Flight Games (by then part of Asmodee), which transferred the RPG to Asmodee's Edge Studio subsidiary.

We've never owned or made money from this game; we created our own house rules to refine the play experience to our own preferences, like a home cook adjusting the spice content of a dish to their personal taste.  We began by taking the original (1st edition) L5R rules and combining them with rules for 7th Sea, another Alderac game.  Because of this, we dubbed our system **L7R**, though these rules are our own creation, taking ideas and inspiration from those and many other tabletop RPGs.

The original books for these games are well worth reading and have great material which any roleplaying group can use.
